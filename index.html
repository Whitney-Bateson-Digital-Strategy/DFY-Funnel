Your Lead Magnet
🧲 What makes a great lead magnet? The best lead magnets give your ideal client a quick win — something that immediately helps them with a real problem — while naturally moving them closer to being ready for your offer. Think of it as a preview of what it's like to work with you: valuable, specific, and leaving them wanting more.
